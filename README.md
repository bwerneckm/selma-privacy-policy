# Selma Personal Agent

Your intelligent personal assistant that helps you stay organized, focused, and in control of your day.

## Features

- **Smart Scheduling** — Automatically organizes your calendar based on priorities and habits
- **Task Management** — Captures, categorizes, and reminds you of tasks at the right moment
- **Daily Briefings** — Morning summaries with weather, agenda, and actionable insights
- **Natural Conversations** — Just talk to Selma like you would a real assistant
- **Cross-Platform Sync** — Seamlessly works across your phone, tablet, and desktop

## Getting Started

1. Download Selma from the [App Store](#) or [Google Play](#)
2. Create your account
3. Grant calendar and notification permissions
4. Start chatting with Selma to set up your preferences
